Executive Overview

In 2025, we asked Austin's creative community to share their experiences with cultural funding programs. Their feedback provides valuable insights that will help us improve these programs together. Through this transparent process, we're committed to addressing challenges and creating more accessible, equitable funding opportunities for all.

This analysis represents the first step in a collaborative process to strengthen Austin's cultural funding programs. By listening to diverse voices and working together, we can build a system that better serves our entire creative community.

Transportation and Access Feedback

🚗 Transportation and Parking: A Key Consideration

62.3% of survey respondents identified transportation and parking as barriers to attending cultural events.

This feedback highlights the importance of considering venue accessibility and transportation options when planning cultural programs. Some neighborhoods face greater challenges than others, suggesting opportunities for targeted solutions and partnerships.

For City Leadership

Based on community feedback, we recommend:

  • Review and improve application processes
  • Enhance communication about funding opportunities
  • Consider dedicated support for smaller organizations

For Cultural Organizations

Your struggles have been heard. While we push for change:

  • Document your system challenges
  • Share this report with your networks
  • Participate in the redesign process

For Individual Artists

Your feedback is valuable. We encourage you to:

  • Continue sharing your experiences and ideas
